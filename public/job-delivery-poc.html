Connect to Rise-X MarineStream

Why connect to Rise-X?

Connecting to Rise-X allows you to:

  • Access vessel data - Pull real-time vessel information, General Arrangements, and inspection history
  • Sync jobs - Jobs you create here will sync to the Rise-X platform, accessible to your team
  • Generate reports - Use Rise-X's reporting templates and document management

Note: A Personal Access Token (PAT) is required to securely connect to Rise-X. You can generate one from your Rise-X MarineStream account settings.
