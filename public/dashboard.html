Performance Scoring Methodology

FON Freedom of Navigation Score

Indicates a vessel's ability to operate freely without biofouling-related restrictions.

FON = 100 - (Œ£ FRi √ó Coveragei √ó Weighti)
  • FRi - Fouling Rating (0-5 scale per IMO guidelines)
  • Coveragei - Percentage of area affected
  • Weighti - Location weight (hull=1.0, niche=1.5, critical=2.0)

HP Hull Performance Score

Current hull efficiency based on fouling state and hydrodynamic impact.

HP = BaseEfficiency √ó (1 - FoulingPenalty)
  • Each FR level adds ~3-5% drag penalty
  • Macrofouling (FR4-5) has exponential impact

Score Ranges

90-100 Excellent - No restrictions
80-89 Good - Minor maintenance recommended
70-79 Warning - Cleaning due soon
<70 Critical - Immediate attention required
